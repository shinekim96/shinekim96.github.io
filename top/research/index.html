Overview of Research Projects

My research interests are in real-time platform architectures and experimentation in 5G and Next G wireless systems.

One of major challenges that we see is that there is a gap between theory and practice. So, the goal of most of my research is to provide feasibility studies for state-of-the-art theoretical advances in wireless communications.
